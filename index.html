Importance of Push-Ups

A push-up is a bodyweight exercise that primarily targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps, while also engaging the core and other stabilizing muscles. It's one of the most effective and versatile exercises for building upper body strength.

Benefits of Push-Ups:

  1. No Equipment Needed
  2. Strength Building
  3. Core Stability
  4. Versatility
